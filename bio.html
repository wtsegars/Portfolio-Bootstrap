About Me

My name is Will Segars, I was born in Atlanta, GA and was raised in Roswell, GA. I graduated from the University of Georgia in 2016 with a major in journalism and two certificates (that acted as a double minor) in music business and new media. While I was in college, I got my first exposure to coding through an introductory web development class that I took as an elective. Not only did I enjoy the class a lot (so much so that it ended up becoming my most favorite class I ever took), I noticed that I was very good at what I was doing in the class. Later on, I decided to enroll in a "Coding Boot Camp" through Georgia Tech in order to hone the skills that I already had and to learn about coding in depth in hopes of pursuing a career in software development.
